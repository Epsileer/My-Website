See All Contests

This home screen will display all the upcoming and past contests (codechef challanges).

Here is where you can show off your computer programming skills. Take part in our 10 day long monthly coding contest and the shorter format Cook-off coding contest. Put yourself up for recognition and win great prizes. Our programming contests have prizes worth up to INR 20,000 (for Indian Community), $700 (for Global Community) and lots more CodeChef goodies up for grabs.

Red dots show the upcoming contests, Green dots show the current contests and Black dots show the past contests.

Get notified for future contests

Now you will get notifications of registered upcoming challenges, events and contests on one click.

The user will get notified regarding the challeges 2 hours before it starts.

The submission will also be notified to the users everytime he/she submits the solution of the challenges.

Practice to horn your skill

Try your hand at one of our many practice problems and submit your solution in a language of your choice. Our programming contest judge accepts solutions in over 35+ programming languages. Preparing for coding contests were never this much fun! Receive points, and move up through the CodeChef ranks. Use our practice section to better prepare yourself for the multiple programming challenges that take place through-out the month on CodeChef.

All the questions are sorted by easy, medium and hard tags.

Profile and Rating

Now you can easily track your global, codechef and country rating. You can compare your profile with others.

In profile section you can view the global and country rating of any user. you can also get anyones basic informaiton like institution, country, language and address.
